# Flashcard Review

## Register
- **Type:** Product + portfolio showcase
- **Role:** A mobile-first flashcard study tool with AI-powered card generation and spaced repetition
- **Positioning:** A study tool that's also a demonstration of craft — every detail intentional, every interaction considered

## Users
- **Primary:** College and high school students preparing for exams
- **Context:** Studying 2-3 sessions before a test, often late at night, on mobile
- **Need:** Fast, focused review — no distractions, no friction, just efficient spaced repetition

## Brand
- **Personality:** Minimal, precise, airy
- **Tone:** Quietly confident. Doesn't shout. Lets the content breathe.
- **Feeling:** Like a well-designed instrument — every element has a reason to exist
- **Anti-brand:** Not gamified, not playful, not cluttered. No badges, no confetti, no cartoon characters.
